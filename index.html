Who I am:

Hello, my name is Angelo Gomez. I'm a Christian college student currently attending Bob Jones University in Greenville, South Carolina. I am a baseball player and have been a baseball player as long as I can remember. I love my family and my friends and I enjoy spending time with my loved ones. I'm a laid-back person and I like to be kind to others. 

Where I'm from:

I am originally from San Diego, California; which I take a lot of pride in. I loved growing up in San Diego as there was always so much to do and I had a really good core group of friends to be around. I moved to Houston, Texas my sophomore year of high school to where I graduated from Clear Springs High School in League City, Texas. Originally, my family traces their origins back to Puerto Rico, something else I also take a lot of pride in.

What I enjoy doing:

I'd say the thing I enjoy doing most is reading my bible alone in my room. There's something special about having 1-on-1 time with Jesus and getting to know him better. After that, I'd say the rest of my top 5 would be spending time with my loved ones, playing baseball, exercising, and playing video games (Not really a big fan of school work, but I see its importance).
